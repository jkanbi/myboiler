About


Most people's heating systems are not running to their maximum 'A Rated' efficiency. They are costing them more than they need to in addition to rising energy costs.

Our mission here at MyBoiler, is to keep people warm using the most suitable and efficient 'A Rated' appliances and products available. The MyBoiler Team is able to install and optimise systems, thereby saving on utility bills and unnecessary expenses.

With a highly experienced team of engineers and speciliasts, MyBoiler is here to 'Keep People Warm'
